A short piece about Colin Mcrae

Colin Mcrae was a Scottish rally driver from Landark, Scotland. He was the youngest person, as well as the first Scottish person, to win the World Rally Championship Drivers' title. Colin Mcrae is the is the son of Jimmy Mcrae Mcrae, five-time British Rally Champion, and brother of rally driver Alister Mcrae.

Colin Mcrae has shown an outstanding performance and achievements throughout his career which consisted mostly of rally driving. He has enabled Subaru World Rally Team to win the World Rally Championship Constructors' title three times in succession, from 1995 to 1997. He was then with Ford Motor Co. team for four years where he won nine events, then moved to Citreon World Rally in 2003. While there he didn't win any events, but he helped them win the first of their three consecutive manufacturers' titles.
